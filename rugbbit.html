How might we give failures hope during the NFT bear market?

During the bear market, most investors will suspend their actions to decrease the loss of money. So, we created a pool to supply investors to exchange their worthless NFTs with us. We will give investors lottery tickets. The winner can take all the tokens away.
This activity can increase those worthless NFT's value. Not only can it warm up the market, but it also can give some hope to the investors.

Roles

Researcher / Commuication / Prototype / NFT Artist
Web3 industry changes really fast. All development must to meeting everyday to make sure the project is on the right track. Also we must to build this project as fast as posible. So, we only have 2 weeks to build up the project before launched. After the PM lead gave me the planning report I started to research the user problems and create the prototype at the same period. The most excited part is to create our own NFT for this project.

Ideate

We are all victims.

We all being played for the suckers by those well known NTFs.

  • Secondary Research
    There are only 16% of investors earn money during the year 2023. Some well-known NFTs started to rug-pull the investors after they bought new NFTs. So, those products became worthless and hard to sell out of hand. And the NFT team just took all the money away.
  • Interview
    We interviewed around ten people who lost money during the bear market. The worst of all was divorced from his wife. Investor's main issue was being cheated by the great blueprints that those NFT teams gave them. Then, they got rugged after investing. After that, they all have lots of worthless NFTs with them.

NFT Arts

Create an useful app for elders.

This rabbit NTF was made from the AI drawing. Then, I used the generated results to modify the type I wanted. We name "Rugbbit" is inspired by the Eastern Rabit and Rug Put.T So my keywords were all around "rabbit, angry, 2D cartoon style, flat design, etc". Then, I combined the two images that we chose into the final results.

First, the concept is about hating well-known NFTs, so I draw the rebit rip off the photo of those prominent. Second, our prize pool is a lotto drawing, so we turn our NFT looks like a ticket. Investors who buy the NFT get the ticket for the price pool game. After the match, the NFT will change to another image, a torn ticket.
